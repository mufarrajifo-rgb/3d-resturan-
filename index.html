✦ The Artisan

Chef Arjun Mehta

"Cooking is the art of making someone feel at home, anywhere in the world."

Trained at Le Cordon Bleu Paris and apprenticed under three Michelin-starred establishments across France and Japan, Chef Arjun brings a philosophy of hyper-seasonal, terroir-driven cuisine to every plate.

His technique marries classic French fundamentals with the vibrant spice traditions of South Asia — creating dishes that are simultaneously familiar and revelatory.

Le Cordon Bleu, Paris Restaurant Joël Robuchon Narisawa, Tokyo
